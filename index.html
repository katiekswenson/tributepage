Annamarie Kearns
1963-2020

A Tribute to my Mother
By: Katie Swenson

A short biography of Annamarie:

  • Anna was born November 1, 1963 in Kearns, Utah
  • Youngest of 6 children, 3 boys and 3 girls
  • Married the love of her life on June 29, 1985
  • Gave birth to 3 girls in 1986, 1989, and 1993
  • Worked in Pharmacies for 25 years
  • Anna was the life of the party, always getting friends together
  • Loved traveling, camping, fishing, and watching her daughters play sports
  • Had 6 grandchildren
  • Passed away from Mesothelioma on August 13, 2020
